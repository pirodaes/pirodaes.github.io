--- layout: default title: About description: "Nice to meet you!" --- My name is Léonie Aonzo, I love color stains on my hands and telling stories through images.

I am an illustrator and aspiring concept artist based in Genoa, Italy.
I graduated from a three-years course at Accademia di Belle Arti di Urbino, with a thesis on the social and cultural impact of videogames.

Since then I’ve been taking up illustration commissions while working on an indie game for mobile. I recently debuted in children illustration with “Like Snow”, a silent book published by Galata Edizioni.
